About Us

For over 20 years, our cozy countryside recording studio has been a haven for musicians, voice-over artists, and anyone looking to record their sound. Our engineer, Jamie, and his wife, Schmida, run the studio nestled in the Bricklieve Mountains in Sligo, next to a charming cottage. We have experience recording a wide range of music genres and other projects, including solo musicians, bands, audio books, and more. Our studio is fully equipped allowing us to capture the perfect sound. . With a focus on creating a warm and inviting environment, our studio is the perfect place to bring your creative vision to life.
